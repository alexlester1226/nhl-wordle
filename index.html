  • If your guess for one of the categories is yellow it depends the category
  • Team: Same division
  • Age: ± 2 years age difference
  • Height: Same foot (Ex. 5-10 & 5-8)
  • Weight: ± 15lbs difference
  • If your guess for one of the categories is black it is none of the above
